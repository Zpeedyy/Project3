Punky cassetes

This video showcases some video cassettes that were repurposed as jewerly. They were made in a workshop in AKC Medika, the cassetes were painted with spraypaints and then connected to metal chains.
All the elements within the video were mine or recolected throughout the whole semester, mixing jewerly, spraycans and posters as background the set was ready.
The video was recorded with an iphone 13 and the lightning used was a lamp and the natural light coming from the windows.
